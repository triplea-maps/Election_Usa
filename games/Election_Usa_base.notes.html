Elections USA 1.0

 

I thought of this game many years ago and had a board make up. But TripleA has make my dreams come true. 

So here is the first version of Elections USA. 

There are three versions I have prepared for your enjoyment.

1. The Base Game- Is the generic version and has more built in edits. Does not have the automatic territory assignment mode. you have 20 turns before the Election Starts[ Battle- everyone goes to war] So to star grab as much land as you can. Be weary of the Democratic Party, they could steel the Election .

2. The 2 player game is a setup of units, some what historical from 2000-2012. Each party stars with the states they have won the election  with the most. The other states are filled with Hecklers. You have 15 turns to fill the states with your units- signs, Recruiters est.. Then the Election will start.  [Battle- everyone goes to war].

3. The Three player game- Much like game two. States are picked randomly,  The Election stars in 10 turns. The Independent player only gets 100 Money points while the Repulicans and Democrats each get 500.   

 

Winning the Game

Each production point represents one Electoral Collage Vote. [ECV] you need 271 ECV to win [Economic Victory] This can be changed as it is Editable. For total victory set the ECV to 532. The republicans always start with Alaska as won. The Democrats always start with Washington D.C, as Won. Capitals can not be taken and are impassable. 

Democratic-Capital Washington D.C,

Republican-Capital Houston, Texas

Independent-Capital Los Angeles, California

The player with the most ECV before or after Election Day [Battle] will win.
